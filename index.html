Four Tiers of Craftsmanship

Peak Compartment

The apex is crowned with a golden brass finial and houses the most intimate storage space, lined with premium velvet. Ideal for statement rings or rare collectible pieces.

  • ✦ Capacity: 3-4 rings or small items
  • ✦ Material: Teal panels with copper edge trim
  • ✦ Design: Pyramid cap with brass finial

Upper Chamber

Chrome cylindrical rods suspend delicate chains and bracelets, preventing tangling while showcasing each piece. Mosaic tile accents frame this tier in classic Art Deco style.

  • ✦ Capacity: 5-6 necklaces or bracelets
  • ✦ Material: Chrome display rods, teal interior
  • ✦ Design: Mosaic tile borders with copper spine

Mid Chamber

The widening mid-section offers generous space with additional chrome rods and sectioned compartments. Store earrings, cufflinks, or watches with magnetic closures.

  • ✦ Capacity: 8-10 pairs of earrings
  • ✦ Material: Chrome rods, mosaic accents
  • ✦ Design: Open-front display with copper trim

Foundation Level

The grand base tier rests on a black decorative stand with ornamental scroll legs. This level provides the most storage space for bangles, brooches, and keepsakes.

  • ✦ Capacity: 6-8 large items
  • ✦ Material: Teal panels, black lacquer stand
  • ✦ Design: Decorative scroll-leg stand with copper inlay
